Story 126 – The Hidden Valley As the sun began to set, the travelers discovered a valley. The village that was hidden below was surrounded by high mountains. They realized that the legend was true because the people spoke a forgotten language. Although the path was steep, they continued their descent. The elder, who wore a long blue robe, welcomed them. He explained that no one had visited them during the winter months. As the storm cleared, the fog began to lift. They climbed to the peak so that they could see the golden fields below.
