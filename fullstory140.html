Story 140 – The Ancient Map The ink was clear although the paper was very old. The man who studied the map was a historian. When they arrived, they found the location of the tomb. The map, which was found in a trunk, showed a hidden door. He told them that the gold was real. Because they were lost, the treasure remained a secret. They followed the path that led into the cave. They entered the dark room because they were very brave. As the sun rose, the adventure began. They knew that they had succeeded.
