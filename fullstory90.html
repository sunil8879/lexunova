Story 90 – The Piano Recital Playing the piano had been her passion. She decided to perform at the town hall. She enjoyed practicing the complex notes. Her teacher encouraged listening to classical music. It is difficult to learn a fast piece perfectly. She stopped playing when her fingers felt tired. She hoped to impress the audience. Focusing requires a lot of concentration. She wanted to memorize every single detail of the music. Sometimes, she was afraid of failing, but she continued to practice. Standing on stage was seeing her dream come true.
