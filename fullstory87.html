Story 87 – The Festival of Gifts The villagers gave the travelers a meal. They offered the guests their cottage for the night. The elder showed us a gem. We bought our mother some fruit at the market. A child handed the lady a flower. The king sent the army a message. It brought the city great relief. She told the children a legend of her ancestors. The musicians played the crowd a song. The baker baked the children a cake. We promised the leader our loyalty.
