Story 69 – Learning to Surf Swimming in the ocean is an amazing experience. I decided to take lessons last summer at the beach. I enjoy balancing on the board, but paddling is quite difficult. My coach told me to swim very hard toward the waves. I kept falling into the water, but I didn't stop trying. It is important to maintain your balance while standing up. I hope to become a professional one day. Surfing gives me a lot of energy. I love feeling the salt on my face.
