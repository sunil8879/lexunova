Story 93 – The Town Legend A strange story is told by the elderly people. Long ago, a castle was built on the cliff. A dragon guarded the treasure inside. A brave knight challenged the beast. The dragon was defeated after a long battle. The gold was given to the poor people of the village. Every year, the victory is celebrated by the town. Children sing songs about the hero. The knight is remembered as a protector. The castle ruins are visited by tourists. They take photos of the ancient stones. The legend has been told for generations.
