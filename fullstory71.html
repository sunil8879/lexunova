Story 71 – My New Robot My new robot can do many things. It will clean my room and it might even cook breakfast. I should be careful with the remote control. You must not push the red button, or it may explode. "I could help you with your homework," said the robot. We ought to charge its battery every night. If it rains, the robot must stay inside. Shall we go to the park? It would be fun to show my friends.
