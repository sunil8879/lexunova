Story 96 – The Mountain Guide You can see the whole valley from here. You must follow my lead to stay safe. We should not stop for too long. It may rain later this afternoon. We ought to reach the camp by noon. You will need a warm jacket tonight. "I could help you with your pack," said the guide. Shall we cross the river? You must be careful on the slippery rocks. We need not rush because we have time. You would like the view at the top. If we are lucky, we might see an eagle.
