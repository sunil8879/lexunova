Story 60 – The Abandoned Mansion We had walked all the way to the old gates. The wind had pushed the door wide open. Suddenly, a bird flew out from the chimney. I heard a strange noise coming from the basement. The old man has lived in this house for fifty years. He gave us a warning before we entered. The water in the well had frozen over the winter. We found the treasure hidden under the floorboards. The sun has risen now, so we must leave.
