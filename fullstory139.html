Story 139 – The Interview The candidate maintained eye contact. She sat with a straight posture. Her smile was very friendly. When the manager spoke, she nodded. She did not fidget with her pen. Her handshake was firm and confident. She used gestures to explain her ideas. Her tone of voice was calm. She dressed professionally for the occasion. Non-verbal cues are very important. The manager showed his interest by leaning forward. The atmosphere was pleasant.
