Story 107 – Learning to Fly Becoming a pilot requires many hours of study. He wanted to fly since he was a child. He practiced navigating in the simulator. The instructor suggested checking the weather report. It is vital to stay calm during turbulence. He avoided flying during the storm. He hoped to earn his license by June. Operating a plane is a huge responsibility. He learned to use the landing gear properly. He didn't mind studying late, but he needed to get some rest. Seeing the clouds below was a dream come true.
