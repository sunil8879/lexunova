Story 150 – The Stage Performance The actor walked with a confident gait. He used his facial expressions to show sadness. He didn't shiver on stage. His posture was heroic. When the lights went down, he bowed. The audience used applause to show they liked it. He looked at the crowd with intensity. His movements were very smooth. He communicated emotionally without any words. The space between the actors was important. Non-verbal interaction made the story real. Everyone admired the performance.
