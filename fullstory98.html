Story 98 – The Street Market I went to the market to buy an orange and a loaf of bread. There were many stalls selling some fresh fish. Every seller was shouting their prices. I didn't see any honey. A few people were waiting for the bus. The sun was high in the sky. I found several interesting things. It was a busy place for a tourist. Both my bags were heavy. I needed a little help. Finally, I took a taxi back to the hotel.
