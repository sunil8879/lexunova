Story 142 – The Space Colony By 2090, humans will have established a base on Mars. They live in specialized pods. Currently, they are searching for water. Last year, a rocket landed from Earth. While it was traveling, the astronauts tested the instruments. They had trained for months. Next month, a new ship will arrive. The crew will be building the new lab. By August, they will have finished the construction. Mars is a cold planet. We have discovered a lot about space lately. People will talk about this for generations.
