Story 77 – The New Stadium A massive stadium is being built in the center of the city. The architect designed the structure to hold fifty thousand people. Modern materials were selected to ensure safety. Workers installed the steel beams yesterday. The project was funded by the local government. Thousands of jobs were created during the construction phase. The mayor announced that the opening ceremony will be grand. A special ribbon will be cut by the guest of honor. People purchased tickets months in advance. The field is covered with high-quality grass. The team practices on the new pitch every morning. Soon, the first match will be watched by fans from all over the world.
