Story 115 – Conditional Tenses (Type 2 & 3) If I won the lottery, I would travel around the world. If I had a private jet, I would fly to Paris for lunch. If she had studied harder, she would have passed the exam. If the team had scored, they would have won the championship. If we lived on the moon, we would be very light. If the sun had shone yesterday, we would have gone to the beach. I would have come to the party if you had invited me. If you could change one thing in the world, what would it be?
