Story 68 – The Mystery of the Blue Vase "Where is the vase?" asked the detective. The butler replied that he had not seen it since morning. The detective asked the maid if she had cleaned the room. She said, "I did not touch anything on the table." "The door was locked," reported the gardener. He claimed that nobody could enter the house without a key. The detective whispered that the case was very strange. He said, "I will find the thief by tonight!"
