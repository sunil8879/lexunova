Story 114 – Passive Voice (Process) First, cocoa beans are harvested from trees. The beans are fermented for several days. Then, they are dried in the sun. The dried beans are shipped to factories. They are roasted at high temperatures. The shells are removed to reveal the nibs. The nibs are ground into a thick paste. Sugar and milk are added to the mixture. Finally, the chocolate is poured into molds. The bars are wrapped in colorful paper. They are sent to stores around the world. Every year, billions of bars are enjoyed by happy customers.
