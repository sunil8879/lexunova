Story 94 – The Space Mission Briefing "We are ready for takeoff," announced the commander. He informed the base that the rocket was on the launchpad. The scientist asked, "Did you check the fuel levels?" The pilot replied that he had done it twice. "Ignition in ten seconds," yelled a voice. The engineer asked the team if they were strapped in securely. The commander shouted, "Go for launch!" He whispered that he could see the stars getting closer. "It is amazing!"
