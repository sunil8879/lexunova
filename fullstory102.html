Story 102 – The Stolen Masterpiece A famous painting was stolen from the national gallery last night. The thieves deactivated the alarm system. No fingerprints were found by the police. The detective is examining the security footage now. A suspicious van was seen near the exit. The gallery is being guarded by elite security officers today. The museum director offered a large reward for information. The news has been broadcast worldwide. Thousands of tips are being received by the hotline. The mystery will be solved eventually. The police will catch the culprits. Soon, the painting will be returned to its rightful place.
