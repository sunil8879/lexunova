Story 51 – The Kingdom's Hero The Knight was the bravest person in the land. Everyday, Sir Alistair patrolled the stone walls. One morning, a mysterious traveler stood near the castle gates. The king noticed that he carried a silver scroll. Suddenly, three dark shadows emerged from the dark forest. The townspeople realized that the hero was running to save them. By sunset, Sir Alistair returned to the village. The entire village cheered because the dangerous beast was defeated. It was a day when everyone celebrated with a feast.
