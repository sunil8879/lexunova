Story 108 – The Forest Rules You must keep the park clean. Visitors should not feed the wild animals. You can find a map at the entrance. It might rain, so you ought to bring an umbrella. A fire will start if you are not careful with matches. "I could hear a wolf last night," said the camper. May I pitch my tent here? You must extinguish the campfire before leaving. We shall depart at dawn. You need not carry heavy logs. If we stay quiet, we could see a deer.
