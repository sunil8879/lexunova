Story 85 – A Day at the Library I found an old book on the dustiest shelf. It was an adventure story about an explorer in the Amazon. Every page was filled with this amazing art. Those maps were hand-drawn. I didn't have any money to buy it. The librarian said that the book was very rare. Some researchers wanted to see it. It was the best day of the week. Both my friends were waiting for me. We didn't see many people there. It was a quiet place for a student.
