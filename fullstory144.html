Story 144 – The Magic Compass If you hold the compass, it will point North. If you spin it, you will get lost. If I had the key, I would open the box. If the explorer walked faster, he would reach the cave. If they had listened, they would have avoided the danger. If the mountain were higher, it would be impossible to climb. I would be happy if I found the gold. If you ask him, he will show you the map.
