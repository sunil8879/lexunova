Story 132 – The Marathon She has run ten miles already. She felt tired but kept moving. Her shoes were worn out. The spectators are clapping loudly. They gave her some water earlier. She has drunk three liters today. The sun is hiding behind the clouds. She saw the finish line in the distance. She had never run so fast. Tomorrow, her legs will feel sore. She will win a gold medal. The race will have made her stronger.
