Story 113 – Clauses (Relative and Subordinate) The man who lives next door is very quiet. Although he is old, he works in his garden every day. When the moon is full, he paints in the attic. The house, which was built in 1920, has a strange attic window. If you look closely, you can see a light. He owns a cat that has no tail. Because he rarely speaks, people think he is mysterious. The stories that the kids tell are quite scary. As we felt brave, we decided to say hello. The man, who smiled at us, smiled at us kindly. We realized that he was very lonely.
