Story 64 – The Stormy Night We turned off the TV because the lightning was scary. The girl who lived next door was hiding under the table. Although we were scared, we felt safe inside the house. The windows, which were very old, rattled in the wind. We stayed awake until the thunder stopped. We lit some candles when the power finally went out in the neighborhood. The cat, which was named Socks, jumped on my lap. When the branch broke, we heard a loud crash. We went to sleep after the rain finally stopped falling.
