Story 147 – The Library Rules You must be quiet in the library. You should not eat or drink here. You can borrow five books. It might be closed tomorrow. You ought to return the books on time. The librarian will help you. "I could read when I was four," said the girl. May I sit here? You must have a card to enter. We shall find the encyclopedia. You need not pay if you are a student. If we study here, we would pass the exam.
