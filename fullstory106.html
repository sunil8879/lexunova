Story 106 – The Busy Market I bought an apple and a loaf of bread. There were many people at the vegetable stall. Every basket was full of this fresh fruit. Those melons looked sweet. I had little money left in my pocket. The merchant said that the cheese was imported. Some tourists were taking photos. It was the busiest hour of the day. Both my hands were full. I didn't see any empty tables. It was a loud environment for a conversation.
