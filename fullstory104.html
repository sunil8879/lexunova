Story 104 – The Job Interview "Why do you want this job?" asked the manager. The candidate replied that she wanted to use her skills in a growing company. The manager inquired, "Can you start next Monday?" She answered that she was ready to begin her duties immediately. "Please sign this contract," said the assistant. The boss asked the new employee if she had any questions about the salary. She exclaimed, "I am so excited!" but the manager whispered that she needed to read the fine print. Finally, he said, "Welcome to the team."
