Story 118 – Modals (Advice and Deduction) "I can't find my keys! They must be in the house," cried Sarah. "You should check your coat pockets." They could be in the car, but the door is locked. It can't be in the garden because I didn't go there. You might have left them at work. "I ought to have been more careful," she sighed. "You need not worry, we will find them." Shall I call the locksmith? We may find them under the sofa. You must not panic. If we search together, we will find them eventually.
