Story 111 – Verb Forms Last winter, we hiked up the mountain. The ground was covered in deep snow. We wore thick coats. The birds have flown south for the season. We saw a frozen lake that had formed overnight. The wind blew fiercely. We are drinking hot cocoa now. It has made us feel much warmer. The fire is burning brightly in the hearth. Tomorrow, we will ski down the slope. We will take many photos. The sun will have set by the time we finish.
