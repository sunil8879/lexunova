JUMBLED UP

1. Jumbled up sentence : mat the cat on sat the.

Corrected sentence : The cat sat on the mat.

Explanation : Standard English sentence structure is Subject (The cat) - Verb (sat) - Prepositional Phrase (on the mat).

2. Jumbled up sentence : store he to the walked.

Corrected sentence : He walked to the store.

Explanation : Subject (He) - Verb (walked) - Prepositional Phrase (to the store) indicating direction.

3. Jumbled up sentence : waiting are they bus for the.

4. Jumbled up sentence : an apple wants she.

5. Jumbled up sentence : table is the book on mine the.
