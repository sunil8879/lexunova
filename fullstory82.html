Story 82 – The Mountain Expedition At the base of the peak, the climbers checked their ropes. The team leader, skilled and brave, encouraged everyone to keep moving. They climbed extremely carefully because the ice was slippery. The man who wore the red jacket was the most experienced in the group. As they climbed higher, the air became thin. They continued the journey even though their legs were tired. The peak, which was covered in snow, was hidden in the clouds. They stood on the icy ledge. Breathing the cold air, they enjoyed the view. When the clock struck five, the sun began to set. The team cheered because they reached the summit successfully.
