Story 81 – The Archaeological Dig Two years ago, the team traveled to the Egyptian desert. They were digging for weeks when they found the entrance. They had never seen such gold. Now, the researchers are studying the ancient scrolls. They examine the ink carefully under a microscope. The professor has just published a new theory. Next summer, they will return to the site. They will be looking for more artifacts throughout the season. By August, they will have excavated the entire tomb. The artifacts reveal a lot about the past. We have learned much from these discoveries. Everyone will talk about this mission for years to come.
