Story 53 – The Time Traveler’s Diary Yesterday, I traveled to the year 1920. People were dancing in the streets when I arrived. I had never seen such old cars before. Right now, I am sitting in the middle of a busy market. I sip a cup of tea while I watch the crowd. Everyone looks very busy today. Tomorrow, I will fly to the year 3000. I think that robots will be doing all the work by then. Scientists will probably have discovered a way to live on Mars. By the time I return, I will have gathered many stories. I ended my journey with a feeling of wonder. The world has always been full of surprises.
