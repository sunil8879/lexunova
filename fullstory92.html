Story 92 – The Mystery in the Attic In the dusty corner, there was a locked trunk. The key which was made of iron was hidden in an old shoe. When we turned it, a cloud of dust rose up. We saw a gold necklace lying among the old clothes. We opened the chest because we were very curious. The letters that were tied in ribbon were yellow with age. Reading the notes, we learned the family secret. As we finished, we heard a noise downstairs. We ran quickly.
