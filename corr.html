GRAMMAR CORRECTION SENTENCES

1. Statement : She don’t like apples.

Mistake : don’t

Corrected sentence : She doesn’t like apples.

Explanation : Subject-verb agreement; use “doesn’t” with “she.”

2. Statement : I am agree with you.

Mistake : am agree

Corrected sentence : I agree with you.

Explanation : “Agree” is a verb, so “am” is not needed.

3. Statement : He go to school every day.

Mistake : go

Corrected sentence : He goes to school every day.

Explanation : Use “goes” with “he” in present simple.

4. Statement : She have finished her homework.

Mistake : have

Corrected sentence : She has finished her homework.

Explanation : Use “has” with “she” in present perfect.

5. Statement : I didn’t went to the party.

Mistake : went

Corrected sentence : I didn’t go to the party.

Explanation : Use base form “go” after “didn’t.”

6. Statement : There is many cars in the parking lot.

Mistake : is

Corrected sentence : There are many cars in the parking lot.

Explanation : Use “are” with plural noun “cars.”

7. Statement : She can sings very well.

Mistake : sings

Corrected sentence : She can sing very well.

Explanation : Use base form “sing” after modal “can.”

8. Statement : He don’t know the answer.

Mistake : don’t

Corrected sentence : He doesn’t know the answer.

Explanation : Use “doesn’t” with “he.”

9. Statement : I am living here since 2010.

Mistake : am living

Corrected sentence : I have been living here since 2010.

Explanation : Use present perfect continuous for “since 2010.”

10. Statement : She is married with a doctor.

Mistake : with

Corrected sentence : She is married to a doctor.

Explanation : Use “married to” not “married with.”

13. Statement : I am boring in this class.
