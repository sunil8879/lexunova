Story 58 – Space Station Rules All astronauts must follow the safety protocols. You must not open the airlock without permission. We might discover alien life on this planet. The commander shall give the signal when it is safe to land. "I could see the Earth from the window," whispered the pilot. You ought to check your oxygen levels every hour. If the engine fails, we would have a problem. May I use the radio now? You need not worry, as the backup system is working.
