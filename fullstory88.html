Story 88 – The Baker's Disaster There was too much flour on the table. Every cake in the oven was burnt. The baker didn't have any time left. A few cookies were saved. The kitchen was a mess. Several assistants were trying their best to help. He needed a little more sugar. Neither of the recipes was working. Many customers were waiting their turn. All the eggs were broken. Both his hands were white. Finally, some magic happened in another bowl.
