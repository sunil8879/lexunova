Story 99 – The Ghost of the Tower Ten years ago, a man saw a light in the window. He was walking past the tower when he heard a scream. He had never felt so scared. Now, the locals are telling stories about a ghost. They avoid the tower at night. The legend has grown very famous. Next week, a team of experts will investigate the building. They will be looking for evidence of the supernatural. By midnight, they will have discovered the truth. The truth will probably be a simple explanation. We have always been interested in mysteries. The tower is old and cold.
