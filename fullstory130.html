Story 130 – The Rescue Mission Yesterday, the team reached the village. They were searching for survivors when the storm hit. They had never seen such destruction. Now, the doctors are treating the injured. They examine medicines carefully. The captain has just sent a message to headquarters. Next week, they will travel to the capital. They will be working all month. By Friday, they will have completed the operation. The survivors are grateful for the help. We have learned a lot about courage. Everyone will remember this day for a long time.
