Story 59 – The Musical Troupe The band is traveling to a new city. Every one of the musicians has a heavy instrument to carry. The singer, along with her dancers, is arriving at the theater. Neither the drums nor the guitar is in tune right now. A group of fans is waiting outside the stage door. The news about the concert is spreading fast on social media. Ten dollars is the price of a poster. Statistics show that more people are coming. Either the manager or the assistants are checking the tickets.
