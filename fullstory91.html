Story 91 – The Abandoned Railway Along the rusty tracks, we found the old tracks. The station, which was built in 1900, was falling apart. As we walked, we heard a whistling sound. We walked very slowly through the tall weeds. We explored the site although the sun was going down. The trains that once traveled here were now only memories. Searching the debris, we found a rusted bell. Before we left, it started to rain. We felt nervous because it was a spooky place.
