Story 67 – The Wishing Well If I had a golden coin, I would throw it into the well. If you close your eyes, you will see a miracle. If the water glowed, it would mean that the wish was granted. If we had arrived earlier, we would have seen the magic. I would be a prince if I found the crown. If the stars shine, the night will be beautiful. If she knew the truth, she would be very surprised. If you work hard, your dream will come true.
