Story 76 – The Witness "I saw the whole thing happen," exclaimed the witness. She told the detective that she had been standing near the fountain when the bell rang. The officer asked, "Can you describe the man in the blue hat?" She replied that he seemed very tall and was carrying a heavy suitcase. "Stop right there!" shouted a voice from the crowd. The detective asked the stranger if he had an identification card in his pocket. The man shouted, "I am innocent!" but the detective whispered that he didn't believe a single word of the story. Finally, the officer said, "Follow me to the station."
