DIRECT & INDIRECT SPEECH



PART I : DIRECT TO INDIRECT

1.

Direct speech : She said, "I am happy."

Indirect speech : She said that she was happy.

Explanation : Present simple becomes past simple.

2.

Direct speech : He said, "I will go to the market."
