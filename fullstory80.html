Story 80 – The King's Coronation The royal crowd gathered early in the grand cathedral. The ceremony made the young prince King. He felt nervous while the crown was being lowered. The heavy golden crown appeared shiny. The people called him Majesty. King Arthur stood tall with great dignity. The cathedral remained silent throughout the morning. The music was magnificent. The citizens declared the day a holiday. Thousands of subjects waited outside the palace. The king is wise. His rule became legendary.
