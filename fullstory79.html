Story 79 – The Olympic Dream Running in the Olympics had always been his goal. He started training when he was only six years old. He worked hard to improve his speed. His coach suggested eating a balanced diet. It is necessary to practice every day, even when it is raining. He avoided staying up late at night. He hoped to win a gold medal for his country. Succeeding requires both mental and physical strength. He learned to control his breathing during the long races. Sometimes, he felt like giving up, but he promised to do his best. Crossing the finish line was the most exciting part of the journey.
