Story 123 – Verb Forms (Irregular) I went to the old library yesterday. I found a book that had fallen behind a shelf. It had been there for years. The librarian told me that nobody had read it. I took it to the table and began to read. The pages felt thin. The ink had faded but I could still see the words. I have read many books, but this one felt special. My interest has grown. Tomorrow, I will return the book. I will tell my friends what I discovered. Knowledge has given me a new perspective.
