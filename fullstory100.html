Story 100 – The Journey's End The tired explorers rested quietly at the end of the road. They had traveled many miles to reach this place. If they had given up, they wouldn't have seen the sunset. The guide gave them a trophy. They felt exhausted. "We did it!" shouted the boy. He told his sister that he was happy. They began celebrating their success. The mountain was conquered by the team. They planted a flag on the summit. The mission can be called a success. Their story will be told forever.
