Story 74 – Under the Ocean Near the coral reef, we saw a giant whale. The submarine, which was painted yellow, moved slowly. As we went deeper, the water became dark. We turned on the lights to see the fish. A fish that had silver scales swam past our window. We were amazed because it was a beautiful sight. On the ocean floor, there was a shipwreck. Rusty cannons lay on the sandy bottom. We took photos while the whale watched.
