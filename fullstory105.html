Story 105 – The Secret Garden Beyond the wall, there lay a garden. The gate, which was rusty, wouldn't open easily. We entered very quietly so that nobody would hear us. The flowers that grew there were incredibly colorful. Because it was old, the fountain was dry. We explored the path even though it was getting dark. The path, which was narrow, led to a stone bench. We sat under the ancient tree. Looking around, we felt peaceful. As the sun set, we decided to leave. We felt happy because we found a magical place.
