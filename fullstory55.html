Story 55 – The Museum Mystery The rare painting was stolen from the gallery. The thief disabled the security cameras before entering. A small window was broken by the intruder. The police searched the scene for clues. A strange footprint was found on the floor. The detective used his magnifying glass to look closer. Witnesses were interviewed by the officer. They saw a dark car driving away. The alarm was triggered too late to catch the criminal. Now, the case is being investigated by the FBI. They hope that the jewel will be recovered. The museum will be reopened tomorrow with new guards.
