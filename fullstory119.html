Story 119 – Direct and Indirect Speech (The Trial) "State your name for the record," ordered the judge. The witness replied that his name was John Doe. The lawyer asked, "Where were you on Friday?" The witness answered that he had been at home watching television. "I am telling the truth!" shouted the man. The prosecutor asked the witness if he knew the defendant. The defendant shouted, "That is a lie!" The judge whispered that he wanted order in the court. "The jury will decide."
