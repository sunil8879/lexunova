Story 52 – The Generous Chef The famous chef decided to cook the king a royal feast. He handed him the golden platter with a smile. Next, he showed the apprentices his favorite silver whisk. The kitchen staff brought him some fresh basil. The waiter offered the lady the dessert menu. After the meal, the guest gave the chef a glowing review. The chef even bought his assistant a new chef’s hat. He taught him the secret ingredient of the sauce. Finally, he served the children a chocolate souffle.
