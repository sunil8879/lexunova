Story 83 – Survival in the Wild If you get lost, you must stay where you are. You should not wander far from the trail. You can use a whistle to call for help. It might get very cold at night. You ought to build a shelter using branches. A small fire will keep the wild animals away. "I could find some dry wood earlier," said the guide. May I use your flashlight? You must keep your clothes dry to stay warm. We shall wait here until morning. You need not worry, as we have enough water. If we are lucky, we could see a rescue helicopter soon.
