Story 101 – The Martian Base In the year 2080, humans had established a colony on Mars. Currently, scientists are testing new ways to grow plants in red soil. They have found water beneath the surface. Yesterday, the oxygen generator malfunctioned. While the engineer was repairing it, he noticed a strange glowing rock. He had never seen anything like it before. Next week, a new supply ship will arrive from Earth. The crew will be waiting at the landing pad. By the time it lands, they will have finished the new laboratory. The commander believes that life exists on Mars. We will know the truth soon. If the data is correct, history will change forever.
