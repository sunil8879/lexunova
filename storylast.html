One chilly morning, a young girl decided to walk to the forest. She carried a small basket filled with berries. On her way, she met a strange stranger who gave her a warning. "Do not wander into the forest after sunset," he said. Ignoring the advice, she ventured deeper into the woods. Suddenly, the wind began to howl. She screamed for help but no one was there. At last, she remembered her grandmother's words and used a compass to find her way back. When she returned home, she promised never to forget a wise man's warning again.
