Story 84 – The Project Meeting The team of engineers is discussing the new bridge. Every one of the members wants to share an idea. The manager, along with his secretary, is entering the conference room. Neither the drawings nor the model is ready for the presentation. A lot of work remains to be done before Friday. The news about the contract is very encouraging. Everyone works very hard. There are several problems to solve. Statistics show that costs are rising. Either the boss or the employees are responsible for the delay.
