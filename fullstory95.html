Story 95 – The Circus Troupe The troupe of acrobats is preparing for the show. Every one of the lions needs a large cage. The clown, along with his monkeys, is walking into the ring. Neither the trapeze nor the ropes are safe today. A group of children is screaming with joy. The news about the circus is spreading through the town. Everyone looks happy. There are many exciting acts. Statistics show that more people are coming. Either the ringmaster or the helpers are checking the lights.
