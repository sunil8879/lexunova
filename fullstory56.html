Story 56 – The School Project "I finished my poster," shouted Mark. He told his teacher that he had worked very hard on it. The teacher asked, "Did you verify the facts?" Mark replied that he had researched everything in the library. "The colors look amazing!" exclaimed Sarah. She commented that the poster was the best in the class. Mark asked Sarah if she wanted to help him present. She said, "I will be happy to help." The principal announced that the winners would receive a prize.
