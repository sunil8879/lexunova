Story 62 – The Messy Room There were many toys on the floor. Every corner of the room was filled with a lot of junk. I didn't have any time to clean it. This pile of clothes belonged to my brother. Several books were missing their covers. Neither of my parents was happy with the mess. I needed a little help. All the windows were dirty. Both my hands were covered in dust. Finally, after some hours, the room looked another way.
